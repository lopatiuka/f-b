Whether you’re in downtown New York or walking the cobbled streets of Barcelona, you can rest assured that you’re going to come across a McDonald’s or another household name just around the corner.

At the same time, people’s tastes are shifting, and people become more health and sustainability driven. So what do these companies do? They innovate! They change their menus, add more healthy and sustainable options and evolve with the shifts in people’s needs.

In the meantime, newcomers come into play and are cementing their place in a constantly evolving global industry.
